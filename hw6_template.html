This map shows how many housesholds received SNAP (Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program) benefits in each county in Kentucky in 2020. The SNAP program helps eligible low-income households and individuals purchase food. In Kentucky, eligibilty for SNAP benefits is based on an individual having a combined checking and savingss account total less than $2,250.
